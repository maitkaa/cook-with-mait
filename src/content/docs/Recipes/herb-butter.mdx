</Callout>

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (250g) unsalted butter, softened
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place softened butter in a mixing bowl.
2. Add all herbs, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix thoroughly until herbs are evenly distributed.
4. Transfer to parchment paper and roll into a log.
5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

<Callout variant="info">
    Store in refrigerator for up to 1 week or freeze for up to 3 months.
</Callout>

## Uses

- Melted over steaks
- Spread on bread
- Seasoning vegetables
- Finishing pasta dishes

## Tips

- Use fresh herbs for best flavor
- Let butter soften naturally, don't microwave
- Chop herbs finely for even distribution
